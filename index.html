Join Our Book Drive!

Join us at our Annual Book Drive! Our book drive supplies children's books to hundreds of families in need. Help us by participating in our event while we help collect, deliver and host events for our community that can help change a child's life.

Volunteers are the heart and soul of The Children’s Book Bank!

You can help us get more books to families in our community. Reminisce about your favorite children’s books and bond with other volunteers at our virtual book cleaning sessions!

Individual volunteers or groups of 6 or fewer volunteers can sign up for one of our virtual book cleaning sessions via Hands On Greater Portland. Register for a virtual book cleaning session on Hands On Greater Portland. You will receive your meeting log in and other information after registering. Conduct a book drive, assemble your own donation from books your family has outgrown, or pick up a box of books from our warehouse. Check out what kinds of books we can accept here.
